Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana (PM-KISAN)
1. What is it? Minimum income support scheme for farmers up to 6,000 rupees a year.
2. Eligibility: All landholding farmers' families in India. 
3. Distribution: 2,000 rupees every 4 months directly to the bank account.

Tomato Early Blight Disease (Tamatar ki beemari)
1. Symptoms: Brown rings or spots on lower leaves.
2. Cause: Fungal infection spreading in warm, wet weather.
3. Cure: Spraying Copper Fungicide or Mancozeb. Avoid overhead watering and keep plants spaced.

Soybean Yellow Mosaic Virus
1. Symptoms: Whitefly infestation leading to yellow leaves.
2. Cure: Uproot infected plants immediately. Use Imidacloprid to kill whiteflies. Do not use generic urea on infected plants.
